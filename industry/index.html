What's Inside

Six things your bar team will actually use.

Every feature below came out of a real conversation with a working bartender. If something doesn't earn its place in a shift, we take it out. What's left is the stuff your team will open three times a week.

01

MENU R&D ON DEMAND

Generate full seasonal menus in minutes, tailored to your spirit inventory, your house style, and the kind of guest walking in your door. The output isn't a random suggestion. It's a draft your bar manager can actually work from.

02

STAFF TRAINING THAT SCALES

Every bartender on your team gets the same flavor logic, ratio guidance, and substitution reasoning. The ramp time for a new hire drops from months to weeks, and your senior bartenders stop being the single point of failure for your program.

03

A PROPRIETARY FLAVOR ENGINE

About 1,400 ingredients scored across compound and taste axes, built by hand over two years. When the system suggests a swap or a riff, it's working from that data, not guessing. It's not perfect, but it's a lot more consistent than the random cocktail generators floating around the App Store.

04

BAR TEAM MODE

On-shift reference for everyone behind the stick. Recipe lookup, sub suggestions when an ingredient is 86'd at 9pm on a Friday, instant riffs when a guest asks for "something like a Negroni but not a Negroni." Designed for the pace of a working bar.

05

RATIO LAB

For teaching palate, not just teaching recipes. Bartenders adjust spirit, citrus, sweet, and modifier ratios and see the balance shift in real time. It's how you turn a bartender who follows a spec into a bartender who understands why the spec works.

06

UNLIMITED USE, FLAT PRICE

Every bartender at the location, all year, no per-seat billing, no usage caps, no surprise invoices at the end of the quarter. One annual price, locked in, covers the whole team. We price it this way because we don't want to be the thing your bar manager has to "ask about" every time she hires someone.
