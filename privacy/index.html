Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

Alchemixt is built and operated by Point Forge Labs ("we," "us," "our"), based in New Orleans, Louisiana. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it. The short version: we collect as little as possible, we never sell your data, and we don't share it with advertisers.

What we collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from those providers. We do not receive or store your password from third-party sign-in providers.

Usage data. We collect basic usage analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve it. This includes which features you use, how often you open the app, and general engagement patterns. We use Google Analytics on the marketing website.

Subscription data. If you subscribe to Alchemixt Pro or Industry, your payment is processed by Apple (App Store), Google (Play Store), or RevenueCat. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not see or store your payment card details.

Content you create. Recipes you save, riffs you generate, check-ins, ratings, photos, and notes are stored in our database so they sync across your devices. Photos are stored in cloud storage.

Ingredient and inventory data. If you use the bar inventory or costing features, the ingredient and pricing data you enter is stored in your account.

What we do not collect

We do not collect your location, contacts, browsing history outside of Alchemixt, or any biometric data. We do not use tracking pixels from social media platforms. We do not build advertising profiles.

How we use your data

Your data is used to operate the app, sync your content, process your subscription, and improve the product. We may send you transactional emails (password resets, subscription confirmations). We will not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in (for example, via the launch notification signup on this website).

Who we share data with

We use the following third-party services to operate Alchemixt:

  • Supabase for database and authentication (hosted in the United States)
  • RevenueCat for subscription management
  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Sentry for error tracking and crash reports
  • Beehiiv for email newsletter signups (marketing site only)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. We do not share your data with advertisers.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data (such as total recipe counts) may be retained indefinitely.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it at any time by emailing hello@alchemixt.app. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

Alchemixt is not intended for use by anyone under 21 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. The app involves cocktail recipes containing alcohol.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify users via email or an in-app notice. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
