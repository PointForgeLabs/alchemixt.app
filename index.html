What It Does

What Alchemixt actually does.

Evolve any classic.

Start with a Negroni, a Daiquiri, anything. Get ten riffs that actually make sense, generated from the compounds in your bottles. Lock the spirits you have. Swap the modifiers you don't.

See why pairings work.

1,400 ingredients plotted across aromatic and taste profiles. Find the ingredient that bridges two flavors. Understand the structure of the drinks you already love.

Build a full menu from your back bar.

Tell Alchemixt what you have and what you want the menu to feel like. Get a balanced, diverse spec sheet ready to cost out and serve.

Who Built This

Built by hand. Bootstrapped in New Orleans.

I'm Jason. I built Alchemixt because the tool I wanted didn't exist, and because I watched too many good bar programs pay consultants for work that should have compounded instead of evaporating after every quarterly refresh. It's bootstrapped, built in New Orleans, and made by hand. The version that took venture money and chased downloads would look completely different. It would probably also be worse.
